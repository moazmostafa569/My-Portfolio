About

I'm a junior frontend developer who came up building real, small projects rather than just tutorials — which means I'm fast on the fundamentals: component structure, state, responsive layout, and talking through trade-offs with a team.

I care about the parts of a UI that are easy to skip: loading states, empty states, keyboard navigation, error messages that actually help. Clients and teams notice that faster than they notice a fancy animation.

I'm reliable, I communicate early when something's blocked, and I write code the next person (often future-me) can actually read.
